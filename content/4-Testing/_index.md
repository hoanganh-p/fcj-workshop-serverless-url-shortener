### 1. Access Website
*   Go to your CloudFront domain (e.g., `https://d2wnt86s7rdbuv.cloudfront.net/`).
*   Register a new account and Login.
![Login Screen](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-Testing/login-screen.png)

### 2. Create Short Link
*   Enter a long URL (e.g., Google Docs).
*   Click **Shorten**.
![Create Short Link](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-Testing/create-short-link.png)
![Create Short Link Result](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-Testing/create-short-link-result.png)

### 3. Verify Redirect
